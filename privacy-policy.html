Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2025  |  Effective: April 23, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the PERPEACE application ("PERPEACE", "the App", "we", "our"), operated by WisePlus Grow. PERPEACE is an AI-powered social media content automation application that creates and publishes wisdom-based content to social media platforms on behalf of its users.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the PERPEACE application, we may collect the following information:

  • Account credentials and OAuth tokens — required to connect PERPEACE to your social media accounts (TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, Threads, X/Twitter)
  • Content preferences — topics, schedules, and settings you configure within PERPEACE
  • Usage data — information about how you interact with the PERPEACE application

2. How PERPEACE Uses Your Information

The PERPEACE application uses collected data solely to:

  • Automate the creation and publishing of content to your connected social media accounts
  • Generate AI-powered wisdom content from public domain sources
  • Maintain your connection to social media platforms
  • Improve the PERPEACE service

PERPEACE does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except as required to operate the service.

3. TikTok Integration

When you connect PERPEACE to your TikTok account, you authorize PERPEACE to access your TikTok account through the TikTok Content Posting API to publish short-form video and photo content on your behalf.

  • PERPEACE only posts content that you have configured or approved
  • PERPEACE does not access your TikTok direct messages, contacts, or private data
  • You may revoke PERPEACE's access to your TikTok account at any time through TikTok Settings → Security → Manage app permissions

4. Data Storage and Security

PERPEACE stores authentication tokens and user preferences securely. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the PERPEACE service.

5. Third-Party Services

PERPEACE integrates with the following third-party platforms to deliver its service:

  • TikTok (Content Posting API)
  • Meta (Facebook and Instagram Graph API)
  • X Corp (X/Twitter API v2)
  • Meta Threads API

Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data PERPEACE holds about you
  • Request deletion of your data from PERPEACE
  • Disconnect any social media account from PERPEACE at any time
  • Opt out of PERPEACE at any time
